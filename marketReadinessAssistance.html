Market Readiness Assistance (MRA) Grant

Overview

Through Grant Trust, small and medium enterprises (SMEs) will receive an international boost with the Market Readiness Assistance (MRA) grant to help take your business overseas.
Eligible SMEs will receive the following support:

  • Up to 70% of eligible costs, capped at #1,00,000 per company per new market from 1 November 2020 to 31 October 2023 that covers:
    • Overseas market promotion (capped at #200,000)
    • Overseas business development (capped at #500,000)
    • Overseas market set-up (capped at #300,000)
  • Each application is limited to one activity in a single overseas market (e.g. market entry, or participation in a trade fair)

Eligibility

Companies should meet the following criteria:

  • Business entity is registered/incorporated in Nigeria
  • New market entry criteria, i.e. target overseas country whereby the applicant has not exceeded #1,000,000 in overseas sales in each of the last three preceding years
  • At least 30% local shareholding
  • Group Annual Sales Turnover of not more than #10 million; OR Company's Group Employment Size of not more than 200 employees.

Eligible employers who fulfil the aforementioned criterias and interested in submitting an application should click the Apply button below
