Small Scale Business Grant


Overview

This Trust Grant program is designed to support small, independent, Nigerian firms. To ensure that only these types of firms receive program funds, all awardees are required at the time of award that the awardee firm meets the size, ownership and control requirements of the program. Eligibility

To qualify for the small scale enterprise grant, you need to:

  • Be a business entity registered and operating in Nigeria.
  • Have a minimum of 30% local shareholding.
  • The business must be over a year old.
  • The business should not be a beneficiary of any exisiting grant programs.
